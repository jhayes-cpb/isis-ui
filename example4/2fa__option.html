Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Enrollment

To help secure your account, the Integrated Station Information System (ISIS) now offers Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) using an authenticator app. 2FA provides an extra layer of protection beyond your password.

You have two options at this time:

Option 1: Enroll in 2FA Now

  1. Download and install an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator) on your mobile device.
  2. Click the Enroll in 2FA button below.
  3. Scan the QR code displayed on your screen using your authenticator app.
  4. Enter the 6-digit verification code from your authenticator app to complete the enrollment process.

Once enrolled, you will use your authenticator app to verify your identity each time you log in.

Option 2: Skip for Now

If you are not ready to enroll, you may choose to skip for now.

Note: 2FA enrollment will eventually become mandatory. We encourage you to enroll as soon as possible to avoid any future interruptions to your access.
